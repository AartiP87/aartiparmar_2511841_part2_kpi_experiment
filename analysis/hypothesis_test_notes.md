# Hypothesis Test

Metric Tested:
Paid Conversion Rate

Null Hypothesis (H0):
There is no difference in paid conversion rate between control and treatment groups.

Alternative Hypothesis (H1):
Treatment group has a higher paid conversion rate than control group.

Test Type:
One-tailed two-proportion test.

Significance Level:
α = 0.05

Decision Rule:
If p-value < 0.05 reject H0.

Business Interpretation:
A statistically significant increase in conversion rate indicates the new onboarding campaign performs better and can be considered for launch.
